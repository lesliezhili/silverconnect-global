# SilverConnect Global Business Model

## 1. One-Page Summary

### Problem Statement
Family members, seniors, and care coordinators struggle to find reliable, personalized senior care services across international regions. Existing solutions are fragmented, slow, and often lack real-time support, local pricing, and trust signals that enable senior care buyers to make fast decisions with confidence.

### Vision
To become the trusted global senior care marketplace where families and care teams can instantly connect with verified local care providers, book services securely, and access empathetic AI-driven customer support.

### Mission
To simplify senior care coordination by offering an end-to-end digital experience that combines local care discovery, transparent pricing, secure booking, verified providers, and intelligent customer support.

---

## 2. Value Proposition

- **For seniors and families:** fast access to high-quality local care services, multi-country pricing transparency, and one-click booking.
- **For providers:** a trusted channel to acquire customers, manage bookings, and receive payments with minimal administrative overhead.
- **For care coordinators:** a single platform to validate service quality, compare providers, and maintain audit trails for bookings and feedback.

---

## 3. Customer Segments

- **Primary customers:** families, adult children, and seniors seeking support services in Australia, China, and Canada.
- **Provider customers:** caregivers, senior care agencies, healthcare aides, and domestic service professionals.
- **Marketplace partners:** local service agencies, health coordinators, and insurance or employer wellness programs.

---

## 4. Revenue Model

- **Transaction fees:** percentage commission on each confirmed booking.
- **Subscription plans:** premium access for providers to promote listings, fast matching, and analytics.
- **Service fees:** convenience or booking fees for customers using premium arrangements, scheduled priority care, or emergency support.
- **Value-added services:** in-app add-ons like care plan management, teleconsultation referrals, and premium support packages.

---

## 5. Solution Offering

### Core Product Capabilities
- Cross-border service discovery for senior care, personal assistance, home maintenance, medical accompaniment, and wellness support.
- Regional pricing with AUD, CNY, and CAD support.
- Integrated Stripe payment workflow and secure refund handling.
- Provider dashboard for service management, scheduling, and performance feedback.
- Dual-language interface: English and Simplified Chinese.
- AI customer service support and emergency response guidance.

### Differentiators
- Combined marketplace and care coordination capabilities tailored for seniors.
- Localized pricing and regional service matching.
- AI-infused customer support to reduce service friction.
- Built-in compliance and auditability through Supabase database controls.

---

## 6. Key Resources & Activities

### Key Resources
- Product platform built on Next.js, Supabase, and Stripe.
- Provider network and service catalog.
- AI customer support workflow.
- Local operations teams in target regions.

### Key Activities
- Onboard verified providers and maintain quality controls.
- Localize service listings, pricing, and support content.
- Operate payments and transaction reconciliation.
- Manage customer support and emergency response workflows.
- Continuously improve platform reliability and trust signals.

---

## 7. Channels

- Direct website traffic to the marketplace.
- Referral and affiliate partnerships with senior care agencies.
- Social media and localized community outreach.
- SEO and content marketing focused on senior care, family support, and wellness services.

---

## 8. Customer Relationships

- **Automated onboarding:** fast signup for customers and providers.
- **AI customer service:** 24/7 support for booking questions, emergency guidance, and issue resolution.
- **Personalized follow-up:** email notifications and booking reminders.
- **Trust-building:** transparent feedback, ratings, and provider verification.

---

## 9. Cost Structure

- Product development and engineering.
- Cloud infrastructure and Supabase hosting.
- Stripe transaction costs.
- Customer support operations and moderation.
- Marketing, partner acquisition, and localized outreach.

---

## 10. Success Metrics

- Bookings completed per month.
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC).
- Customer lifetime value (LTV).
- Provider retention and fill rate.
- Support resolution time and satisfaction score.
- Service rating average and repeat booking rate.
